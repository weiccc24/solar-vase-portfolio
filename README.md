Seattle's cloudy, rainy climate leaves indoor plants with limited natural light. Our Solar Vase combines an intelligent rotating mechanism with the aesthetic language of traditional Eastern vases. It automatically tracks available sunlight, providing plants with balanced light exposure throughout the day, thus mitigating the risk of disease and improving long-term growth.

The core of the system is a precision-engineered base housing a Light Sensor, a Motor, and a Four-Gear Drive System. This mechanism allows for slow, silent, and accurate rotation, keeping the Light Sensor perpetually aligned with the strongest light source.
